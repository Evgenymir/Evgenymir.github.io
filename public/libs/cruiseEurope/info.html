Explore the heart of Russian culture

St. Petersburg is the real cultural and historical capital of Russia that has plenty of attractions to suit the tastes of every tourist.

The city has more than 300 years of history. It was a witness of glory of sea victories, palace wars, great revolution and other turning point of Russia’s past. The city brought up world-famous poets, writers and artists, intellectuals and politicians.

Dubbed Russia’s ‘northern capital’, St. Petersburg is an open-air museum famous for its numerous bridges, rivers and canals, parks, palaces, museums, theatres, bars, nightclubs and restaurants.

You have a great chance & two options to explore the most famous sights of St. Petersburg.

To visit the residence of Russian Imperator’s family palace and one of the world-famous museum - take the Hermitage tour (see more information)

To see the unique Russian jewelry – take the Amber Room & Faberge tour (see more information)
