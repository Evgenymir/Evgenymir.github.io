24 to 26 april 2018

Speakers

Uwe Beckmeyer

Parliamentary State Secretary, Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy

Uwe Beckmeyer holds the office of Parliamentary State Secretary at the Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy and is the German Government’s Maritime Coordinator. Mr. Beckmeyer, who was born in Bremerhaven, has been a Member of the German Bundestag since 2002.

From 2004 to 2011 he was spokesman for transport policy; since 2011 he has been coordinator for maritime affairs in the SPD parliamentary group in the German Bundestag. From 1987 to 1999 Mr. Beckmeyer was a Member of the Senate of the Free Hanseatic City of Bremen.

Harald von der Osten-Sacken

Vice President Destination Management Sourcing Costa Group

Harald von der Osten-Sacken joined AIDA Cruises in 1998 after being self-employed in tourism for eight years. Harald is currently in the position of the Vice President Destination Management Sourcing of Costa Group.

Costa Group comprises of the brands AIDA Cruises, Costa Crociere and Costa Asia with offices in 14 countries. We are operating 27 ships in about 300 ports and we are proud to be the leader in all the major Continental European markets: Germany, Italy, France, Austria, Switzerland and Spain. Costa Asia is the first international cruise line entered in China in 2006 to operate homeport cruises. Harald worked on AIDA vessels for more than 4 years. Since 2002, Harald continued his career in the AIDA Headoffice in Rostock, Germany in various leadership roles like Destination Management, Port Operations, Hotel Operations and Procurement.
